Happy new year 2014

The GPAC Licensing team wishes you a happy new year. May 2014 bring you fun, adventure, and great open-source multimedia services!

We'd like to thank all the GPAC contributors, the people who made GPAC Licensing possible, and our customers. 2013 was a tremendous year. GPAC was added HEVC, 4K, WebVTT, more live features (e.g. DashCast), Common Encryption...

Stay tuned for more news. 2014 is going to be full of surprises!
